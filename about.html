OUR STORY

V.V. Iron & Steel Co. Pvt. Ltd. is part of IMC Group Company. This company was promoted by Mr. S. Chandresan, an industrialist, in the year 2007 and produced MS pencil ingots for steel industry. In the year 2013, the company stopped production and went for modernization and set up a New Induction Furnace with Continuous Casting Machine for casting MS Billets and a high speed fully automated straight Steel Rolling Mill with a production capacity of 10000 MT TMT Rebars. The Company also has a full-fledged in house sophisticated laboratory for maintaining high quality standards of its products.

OUR MISSION

There are no major producers of TMT Steel in South Tamil Nadu and customers depend on outside supplies for their requirement. Therefore the company’s goal is to produce and serve High Quality TMT Steel bars to the consumers in the Region, with timely delivery and lower transport charges. The company also endeavours to provide employment to local populace and to contribute to the economic development of the region.
