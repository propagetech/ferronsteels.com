CCM

Our own billet produced through Continuous Casting Machine (CCM). The only company to get IS 2830 License for Billet making in South Tamil Nadu.

CONSISTENCY

Produced using PLC online technology to maintain uniform thermo mechanical treatment of each bar.

LOCATIONAL ADVANTAGE

Being located in Tuticorin, ensures speedy delivery, lesser transport cost, protect the customer from price volatility.

EXCELLENT SERVICE

Rebars stored in covered shed, always fresh and professional dispatch team.

QUALITY

Each and every heat of the billets is tested in house and our TMT rods are manufactured in 12-metre lengths as per international standards.

INDUSTRY STANDARDS

Produced IS standards. Granted IS License IS 1786.

BENDABILITY, WELDABILITY AND BONDING

The bars contain low carbon and high manganese, this ensures easy weldability. The bendability is also high as it makes the steel very ductile. The special design of the bars provides excellent bonding between the bar and concrete for superior life.

CORROSION, EARTHQUAKE AND FIRE RESISTANCE

Due to the special Quenching process, Ferron Bars are highly corrosion resistant and suited for coastal areas. High ductility and elongation make these bars earthquake resistant and suitable for use in seismic zones 4 and 5. Due to its composite structure, it resists huge load and can also withstand high temperature ensuring safety.
